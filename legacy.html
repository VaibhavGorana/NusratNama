Life Timeline

1948
Early Life & Roots

Born on 13 Oct,1948 in Faislabad(Paskitan) , in a family lineage of Qawwals from past 600 years . In the ancestral family of Qawwals , Nusrat Sahab was not gifted with good voice - according to his father Fateh Ali Khan .His father wanted to make him a doctor ,Nusrat sahab has his interest in qawwalis and music .On his persistance , his father taught him Tabla when he was very young . Its hard to bilieve that Nusrat sahab used to cry for his bad voice in childhood , but his persistant practice to improve made him rise .

“Mia sahab! Mai isko Ilm deke kya karunga , isko toh Allah ne awaz hi nahi di .” -His Father
1964 - 1971
Breakthrough Performances

After his father's death in 1964 , Young Nusrat continued his Learnings under Ustad Mubarak Ali Khan and Ustad Salamat Ali Khan .This period was all about rigorous Riyaz & classical foundation . He then also GAve his first Life performance on his father's Barsi ,which impressed many other Ustad.Later with continued riyaz , Nusrat sahab gave his first offical public performance in year 1971 at Jashn-e-Baharan festival in pakistan .

“Now there is no stopping of him in Qawwwali.”
1979-1985
International Recognition

In International Debut(Festival of world of Sacred Music,1979) , Nusrat introduced Qawwali to western audience for the first time . His electrifying performance made him a Gloabl ambassador of Sufi music .In 1980 ,Nusrat Sahab was invited in India on wedding of Rishi kapoor , thats when he first performaned in Inda . Later he sung one of most famous qawwali in India -Sanson Ki Mala , Inspired by Saint Meera bai .In 1985, his collaboration with Peter Gabriel invited to perform at WOMAD Festival in London and later collaborated on soundtracks like The Last Temptation of Christ.This was the turning point in taking Qawwali to mainstream International music .

“In western world ,Peter Gabriel is the one who introduced me . We have worked together a lot .” -NFAK
1990
Fusion Era

Now Nusrat Sahab had become a global Sensation , his contribution to bollywood songs ,collab with many famous artist -Michael Brook , in which they released album -Dum Mast Mast - which made even the westerners dance and become gloabl hit . He is invited by many countries , even though westerner didnt understand whats the meaning , yet the music says all .He has still great fans following in Japan , England , France and many other countries .His voice is consiered top 1 among the 40 most famous artist of the world .

“I practiced riyaz 12+ hours everyday for 25 years (which he thought only just build his base ,like warmup) .” -NFAK
1995 -1996
Peak Influence & Recognition

Peak global performance and recognition , performed at venues like ,Royal Albert Hall , Central Park & Carnegie Hall .Time magazine hailed him as the"Voice from Heaven"

“He is like our God , the famous laughing buddha of japan -whoes only message was laughter and joy” - Japanese
1997
Legacy & Contiued Influence

Passed away in 1997 , leaving behind over 125 albums &500+ recordings with many world records and legacy. To this day ,NFAK remains the world's most celebrated Qawwal .

“Mar jau.. toh kabar pe bhi likhna Ali-Ali .” -NFAK
